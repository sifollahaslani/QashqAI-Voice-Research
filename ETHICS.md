# Ethics & Responsible AI

QashqAI Voice is committed to ethical, transparent, and responsible AI practices.

## Core Principles
- Respect for cultural ownership and community consent
- Protection of endangered and minority languages
- No exploitation of cultural or linguistic data
- Human-centered and inclusive AI design
- Transparency in data usage and AI-generated outputs

## Data & Consent
All language data, voices, and oral histories are collected only with informed consent and respect for community rights.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
The project prioritizes accessibility for marginalized communities and people with disabilities.

## Governance
Human oversight is maintained in all AI-assisted processes.
