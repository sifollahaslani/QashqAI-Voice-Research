# QashqAI-Voice-Research
QashqAI Voice – A cultural-tech project for preserving endangered languages, digital inclusion, and ethical AI. Founder: Siefollah Aslani.## About the Project
QashqAI Voice is a research-driven cultural and technological initiative focused on
the preservation of endangered and minority languages through ethical and inclusive AI.

## Core Areas
- Endangered & minority languages
- Voice AI and speech technologies
- Cultural heritage & oral history
- Digital inclusion & accessibility
- Ethical and responsible AI

## Geographic Context
Germany – Bremen & Hamburg  
International collaboration-oriented
